Vision Zero App

A map of all submissions to the DC Government's Vision Zero application filterable by type of submitting commuter. The Vision Zero Safety data comes from a web-based application developed to allow the public to communicate the real and perceived dangers along the roadway from the perspective of either a pedestrian, bicyclist or motorist.
